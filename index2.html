About Our Venue

A Premium Destination for Celebrations

Nestled in the heart of the city, Grand Event Lawn offers an exquisite outdoor venue for hosting celebrations of all scales. With over a decade of experience, we have perfected the art of creating magical moments that last a lifetime.

Our 5-acre lush green property features beautifully manicured lawns, elegant floral arrangements, and stunning lighting designs that transform ordinary events into extraordinary experiences. We specialize in Indian weddings and traditional celebrations, offering authentic decor and facilities.
