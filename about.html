About Shubh Lawn Events

Creating Unforgettable Memories Since 2010

Our Journey

What started as a single lawn in 2010 has now transformed into Mumbai's premier event destination.

Founded by Mr. Rajesh Sharma, Shubh Lawn Events was born out of a vision to provide a perfect blend of traditional Indian charm and modern amenities for celebrations. With a background in hospitality spanning over 20 years, Mr. Sharma noticed a gap in the market for venues that truly understood the intricacies of Indian celebrations.

Our first event was a small wedding with just 200 guests. Today, we host over 50 events annually, ranging from intimate gatherings to grand celebrations with 2000+ guests.

"Har celebration ek kahani hai, aur hum uss kahani ko yaadgaar banana chahte hain."

- Rajesh Sharma, Founder
